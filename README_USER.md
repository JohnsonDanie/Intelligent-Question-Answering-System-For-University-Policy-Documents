# 🏛️ Nile University Policy AI - User Guide

## Welcome!
The **Nile University Policy AI** is an intelligent assistant designed to help students and staff navigate university rules, grading policies, and conduct guidelines instantly. Instead of searching through hundreds of pages in a PDF, you can simply **ask a question** and get an answer rooted in official documents.

---

## 🌟 Key Features
- **Natural Conversations**: Ask questions like "What happens if I miss an exam?" or "How do I apply for honors?" in plain English.
- **Instant Search**: The AI reads through all uploaded university handbooks and policies in seconds.
- **Trust & Verification**: Every answer comes with a **"View Sources"** button. Click it to see exactly which document and page number the information was pulled from.
- **Privacy First**: Your questions and policy documents are handled securely within the Nile University environment.

---

## 🚀 How to Use the System
1. **Upload Policies**: If you are an administrator, use the **"Upload Policy"** button on the left sidebar to add PDF handbooks.
2. **Ask a Question**: Type your question into the chat box at the bottom and hit enter.
3. **Review the Answer**: The AI will provide a concise summary. 
   - *Note*: If the answer isn't in the policy, the AI will tell you to **Contact the Registrar**. This ensures you never receive incorrect or "guessed" information.
4. **See the Proof**: Click "View Sources" to see the relevant snippets from the official document.

---

## 💡 Tips for Better Answers
- **Be Specific**: Instead of "Grading," ask "What is the minimum grade required for a pass in Engineering?"
- **One Topic at a Time**: Ask one clear question for the most accurate response.

---

## 📞 Support
If the AI directs you to the Registrar, please reach out to the **Nile University Registrar's Office** for personalized assistance on complex cases.
